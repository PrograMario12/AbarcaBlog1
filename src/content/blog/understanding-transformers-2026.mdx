# Understanding Transformer Architectures

Transformers have revolutionized the field of Artificial Intelligence. Since the seminal "Attention Is All You Need" paper, the architecture has become the de facto standard for everything from natural language processing to computer vision.

## The Evolution of Attention

Over the years, the way we handle attention has scaled. We moved from standard self-attention to sparse attention, flash attention, and linear attention mechanisms. These improvements have allowed us to process contexts of millions of tokens, unlocking entirely new capabilities for agentic AI.

## Why It Matters for Software Engineers

As a software engineer, integrating these models requires understanding their bounds. Token limits, inference costs, and reasoning capabilities dictate the system architectures we build around them.

> AI isn't just a feature anymore; it's a fundamental system paradigm analogous to client-server architectures.
